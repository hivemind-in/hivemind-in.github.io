Before you apply

What will be expected of you

HiveMind is a virtually run research lab, that is, all our work happens remotly. In order to keep track of the progress you are making we expect you to maintain the README.md file of our github repo and update it on a weekly basis. Part of evaluation will be based upon the work done every week.

Furthermore, the code-base of the project must be maintained on our Github repo. We expect you to update the repo atleast once a week together with the README.md file. The evauation will heavely be dependent on the github repo.

The interns will make use of open-source tools and freely available technologies such as Google Colab to build the end-to-end pipeline. You are expected to have worked with jupyter notebooks and/or kaggle kernels

What you get out of this internship

Through this internship you get to work with real-world problems that can be solved using deep learning. At the end of the internship you and your mentor publish a joint paper in an international journal and convert the project into an android application. If the app is good enough we publish your app on the play store and you get 70% of the revenue while the mentor gets the remaining 30% for 1 year from the point of publication of the application. HiveMind will not pay any direct stipend to the mentor or the intern other than the revenue generated from the app.

HiveMind bears the entire cost of the publication of the paper and the application(optional). The interns don't have to (and will never be asked to) pay for it. In case of publication of the research paper in a conference HiveMind will not bear travel or living expenses for you or the mentor.

Duration and breakdown

Duration: 2 Months 10 days (present batch May 2020)

Breakdown:

First 10 days: Proposal and literature survey

Next 25 days: Learning and development of the deep learning end-to-end pipeline

Next 15 days: Writing research paper

Next 20 days: Development of the android app with flutter/react-native (optional)

The interns must report to the mentors in a standup call on skype atleast thrice/week

The interns are expected to spend atleast 21hrs/week on their project
